Lists are nice. I'm a big fan of "best of" lists as they help me keep things in one place for future reference. By viewing lists compiled by other people you can get new ideas and refine your own lists. It's fun and useful. This time, I've put together a list of restaurants in Brussels that I particularly like. This is not exactly a "best of" list. It's merely a collection of restaurants I've found to be good and not too expensive. In Brussels you can't have a bad meal even if you wanted to!

Brussels restaurants by cuisine

  • Belgo Belge, 20, Rue de la Paix, 1050 Brussels

Belgian essentials

Fritkots

  • Chez Martin, Place Madou (Saint-Josse-ten-Noode). The best friterie in Brussels and in the world! Period. Ask for sauce mafia, and you'll be in heaven. Update: Martin retired in December 27, 2009 - a sad day for all frit lovers. Thanks Martin for your dedication and high quality frits, all the best for future!

Beer shops

  • Le Bier Circus, 57, Rue de l'Enseignement, 1000 Brussels
  • Beer Mania, 174-176, Chaussée de Wavre, 1050 Brussels

It goes without saying that there are countless other good—and better—restaurants in Brussels that are not mentioned here. Go find out yourself! Bon appétit! Smakelijk!
