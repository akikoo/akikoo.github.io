Prince played two concerts in Copenhagen on October 20, 2010. His latest album is called 20Ten. A coincidence?

I was lucky to get a ticket for his first show that day at Forum, Copenhagen. (The second, surprise gig was at Amager Bio.)

I’m enlightened; there’s no other way to describe it. I’ve seen Prince!
