In September 2012 Voyageur Press published a new book, The Big Lebowski: An Illustrated, Annotated History of the Greatest Cult Film of All Time. I love the movie and I'm honored that they used one of my photographs of the Lebowski Bar in Berlin (with my permission) in the book (p. 193).

It's an entertaining and informative book about a great movie that makes me laugh every single time I watch it, again and again. Mark it eight, Dude!
