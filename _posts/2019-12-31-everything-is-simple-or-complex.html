It's becoming increasingly challenging to navigate the highly complex technology product landscape (not to mention the world!).

How we choose to look at these problems/challenges is up to us. Zoom in and zoom out of the challenge - perhaps the solution is just outside where you're currently looking :) You can read more in Tanner Christensen's great post Everything is as simple or complex as you want it to be.

We'll have plenty of challenges and opportunities in the next decade where critical thinking will continue to be a crucial skill.

Happy New Year 2020! :)
