AI is moving fast, and it’s largely being shaped by a handful of large American commercial companies with their own interests. That’s worth keeping in mind.

But I’d rather engage with it critically and create a foundation to build on, than dismiss it. Ignoring it is not an option.

Knowing how it works, how to apply it in real workflows, what it can and can’t do, and where the risks lie feels more useful than either hype or dismissal.
