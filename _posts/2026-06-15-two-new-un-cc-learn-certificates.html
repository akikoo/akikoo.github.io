I just received my certificate in the course An Introduction to Climate Change and Human Rights at UN CC:Learn. A couple of days earlier, I also completed Net Zero 101: What, Why and How.

Both were short, but they gave me a clearer picture of how climate change connects to the way we live, work, and treat each other: from emissions pathways to Net Zero, to the human impact of a changing climate, particularly on the most vulnerable.

I’ve been trying to live climate-conscious in my own daily choices, and courses like these are a small but useful way to turn that curiosity into a better understanding of the bigger picture. Always looking to keep learning and find ways to do things a little better.
