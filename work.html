Experience summary

Building on my long international experience in value creation through teamwork, inclusive UI systems and web accessibility, I am now (since many years) focusing on Agile methodologies, cross-cultural communication and servant leadership.

Rather than concentrating on a particular technology, my main interest as Scrum Master is people, teams and the wider organisation, to continuously improve and become more effective together.

My current ideal work setting is at an international, distributed, remote-first (WFA), product-led and value-driven organisation in Malmö (Öresund) region, or remote.
