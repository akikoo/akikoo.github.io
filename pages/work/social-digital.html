After building the new Social Digital website, in was hired in fixed-term projects for Social Digital and Diversa to evaluate, audit and advise the teams on accessible web design and development, starting all the way from wireframes up until delivery.

I ran workshops on sustainable web accessibility process, helping teams avoid common pitfalls and creating awareness about issues and barriers in inclusive product design and development.
